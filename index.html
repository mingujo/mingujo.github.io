About Min

Hi, my name is Min Gu (Min) Jo, a senior undergraduate with a double major in Statistics and Economics, and a minor in Computer Science at UC Berkeley, expecting to graduate in December 2016. I believe data provide us a way to solve unstructured problems with the power of data analysis/science.

My independent research experience supervised by the professor at Department of Statistics, and other related experiences including projects and internship allowed me to develop a sincere interest in the field. To broaden my interest, I have taken Andrew Ng’s Machine Learning course and currently working on a Kaggle competition to actively experience how to apply non-linear algorithms on large-scale information.
